'''Norbert Leser''' (May 31, 1933 – December 31, 2014) was an [[Austria]]n [[jurist]], [[political scientist]] and [[social philosopher]] best known for his lifelong affiliation with, and critical work on, the [[Social Democratic Party of Austria]] and [[Austromarxism]] in particular. He was born in [[Oberwart]] and died in [[Eisenstadt]].
