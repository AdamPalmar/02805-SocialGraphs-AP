'''Jenny Teichman''' is an Australian/British [[philosopher]], writing mostly on [[ethics]]. She was born in [[Melbourne]], [[Australia]] in 1930 and has taught mostly at [[Murray Edwards College, Cambridge]], formerly known as New Hall, where she is now an Emeritus Fellow.<ref name="ref1">[http://www.infidels.org/library/modern/finngeir_hiorth/teichman.html Book Review]</ref> She taught for shorter periods in Australia, Canada and the USA.
