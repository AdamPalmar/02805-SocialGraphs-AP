'''Anne Sjerp Troelstra''' (born 10 August 1939) is [[Emeritus]] professor of [[pure mathematics]] and [[foundations of mathematics]] at the [[Institute for Logic, Language and Computation]] (ILLC) of the [[University of Amsterdam]].

He is a [[Constructivism (mathematics)|constructivist]] [[logician]], who has been influential in the development of [[intuitionistic logic]].<ref>[http://www.daimi.au.dk/~dp/Arkiv/Oktober2001/profil.html Daimiposten &ndash; Oktober 2001<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><ref>[http://www.ozsl.uu.nl/MartinLob.html MARTIN LÖB (1921&ndash;2006)<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061223222900/http://www.ozsl.uu.nl/MartinLob.html |date=December 23, 2006 }}</ref> With [[Georg Kreisel]], he was a developer of the theory of [[choice sequence]]s.<ref>{{Citation |first=Mathieu |last=Marion |title=Wittgenstein, Finitism, and the Foundations of Mathematics |year=1998 |location=New York |publisher=Oxford University Press |page=205 |isbn=0-19-823516-X }}.</ref> He also wrote one of the first texts on [[linear logic]],<ref>{{Citation |first=Herman Ruge |last=Jervell |title=Review: ''Lectures on Linear Logic'' by A. S. Troelstra |journal=Journal of Symbolic Logic |volume=61 |issue=1 |year=1996 |pages=336–338 |doi=10.2307/2275616 }}.</ref> and with [[Helmut Schwichtenberg|H. Schwichtenberg]] he co-wrote an important book on [[proof theory]].<ref>{{Citation |first=Roy |last=Dyckhoff |title=Review: ''Basic Proof Theory'' by A. S. Troelstra; H. Schwichtenberg |journal=Journal of Symbolic Logic |volume=63 |issue=4 |year=1998 |pages=1605–1606 |doi=10.2307/2586674 }}.</ref>

He became a member of the [[Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences]] in 1976.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=https://www.knaw.nl/en/members/members/4881 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160214194705/https://www.knaw.nl/en/members/members/4881 |title=Anne Troelstra |publisher=Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences |date= |archive-date=14 February 2016 |accessdate=14 February 2016}}</ref>
