'''John Milton Cooper, Jr.''' (born 1940) is an American [[historian]], [[author]], and [[educator]]. His specialization is late 19th- and early 20th-century American Diplomatic History. Cooper is [[Professor Emeritus]] at the [[University of Wisconsin-Madison]].<ref>[http://history.wisc.edu/emeriti_jc.htm University of Wisconsin&ndash;Madison]</ref>

His most recent book, ''[[Woodrow Wilson: A Biography]]'', was published in 2009.
