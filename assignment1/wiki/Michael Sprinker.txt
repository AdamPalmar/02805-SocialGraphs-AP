'''Michael Sprinker''' (8 February 1950 in [[Elgin, Illinois]] – 12 August 1999) was a literary critic known for his writings on [[Louis Althusser]], [[Walter Benjamin]] and [[Bertolt Brecht]], among others, as well as for his editorial work at [[Verso]], [[Cambridge University Press]], the ''[[New Left Review]]'' and ''[[The Minnesota Review]]''.  He also taught at [[Oregon State University]]<ref>[http://clogic.eserver.org/3-1&2/wald.html Untitled Document<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> and the State University of New York at Stony Brook.
