'''Phillip H. Wiebe''' (born 1945) is the former Chair of the Department of Philosophy, Professor of Philosophy and former Dean of Arts and Religious Studies at [[Trinity Western University]] and its School of Graduate Studies. He is the author of ''God and Other Spirits'' and ''Visions of Jesus,'' both from [[Oxford University Press]]. His primary areas of research are in philosophy of religion and science, and epistemology.

==Degrees==
He received a B.A. and  M.A.. from the [[University of Manitoba]], and a  Ph.D. from the [[University of Adelaide]].
*Studied at Adelaide under notable scholar [[J.J.C. Smart|J. J. C. Smart]],  .

==Areas of expertise==
Analytic philosophy; philosophy of religion, religious experience. 
*Some have described Phillip Wiebe as a "Mystical Empiricist." 
*Also a noted [[Shroud of Turin]] expert.
