'''Norbert Bolz''' (born April 17, 1953) is a [[Germany|German]] [[philosopher]] and [[media theory|media theorist]]. He is a professor at the [[Technical University of Berlin]].

Bolz developed a media theory, the ''"{{lang|de|Theorie der neuen Medien}}"'', that is influenced by [[Friedrich Nietzsche]], [[Walter Benjamin]] and [[Marshall McLuhan]].
