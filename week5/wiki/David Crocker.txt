== Education ==
David Crocker gained his bachelor's degree in psychology from [[DePauw University]], [[Greencastle, Indiana]], in 1959. He also has three degrees from [[Yale University]] ([[Master of Divinity|M.Div]] in Philosophy of Religion 1963, [[Master's degree|MA]] in Philosophical Theology and Philosophy of Religion 1965, and a [[Doctor of Philosophy|Ph.D]] in Philosophical Theology and Philosophy of Religion 1970).<ref name=CV />

== Academic career ==
* Taught philosophy for 25 years at [[Colorado State University]]<ref name=CV />
* UNESCO Chair in Development at the [[University of Valencia]].<ref name=UNESCO>{{Citation | last = Morris | first = Christopher | contribution = Contributors | editor-last = Morris | editor-first = Christopher | title = Amartya Sen | pages = xi | publisher = Cambridge University Press | location = Cambridge New York | year = 2009 | isbn = 9780521618069 }}</ref>
* Fellow of the [[Human Development and Capability Association]].<ref name=HDCA>{{cite web|title=HDCA fellows|url= https://hd-ca.org/about/hdca-fellows|publisher=Human Development and Capability Association|accessdate=8 July 2014}}</ref>
