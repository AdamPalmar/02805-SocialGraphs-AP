'''Isaac Richard Jay Malitz''' (born 1947, [[Cleveland, Ohio|Cleveland]], [[Ohio]]) is a [[logic|logician]] who introduced the subject of [[positive set theory]] in his 1976 [[Doctor of Philosophy|Ph.D.]] Thesis at [[University of California, Los Angeles|UCLA]].
